Health Renewal

Health insurance plans have a predefined validity, typically ranging from one to three years. Failure to renew your health insurance policy on time can result in the loss of accrued benefits, including waiting period benefits and no claim bonuses.

With Aapka Policywala, renewing your health insurance policy is fast and easy. Ensure continuous coverage and retain valuable benefits by renewing your health insurance policy in a few steps. We have partnered with the best health insurance companies of India to simplify renewal for you. 

Don’t risk losing out on essential medical coverage—renew your health insurance today to safeguard your health and financial well-being.

Benefits of Renewing Health Insurance Plan

Seamless Continuity

Renewing your health insurance plan ensures uninterrupted coverage, safeguarding you against unexpected medical expenses and healthcare costs throughout the renewed policy term.

Tax Advantages

Renewing your health insurance policy allows you to continue enjoying tax benefits under Section 80D of the Income Tax Act. Premiums paid remain eligible for tax deductions, contributing to your overall financial planning.

No-Claim Bonus Accumulation

Many health insurance plans offer a valuable No Claim Bonus (NCB) for every claim-free year. This bonus leads to an increase in the sum insured without any corresponding increase in the premium amount.

Access to Cashless Facilities

 Renewing your health insurance plan ensures continued access to cashless treatment facilities at network hospitals. Cashless claims streamline the hospitalisation process by eliminating the need for upfront payment.

Renewing Health Insurance: Critical Points

Before renewing your health insurance policy, it’s crucial to conduct a comprehensive review to ensure you’re making informed decisions about your coverage. That’s because the health insurance industry is competitive and new additions keep coming. Here are key factors to consider:

Critical Points Factors to Consider
Policy Coverage and Benefits Evaluate if your current policy adequately meets your healthcare needs and offers sufficient protection against potential medical expenses.
Premium Amount Assess the renewal premium to ensure it fits within your budget and compare it with other policies in the market for the best value. Policy transfer is super easy too!
No-Claim Bonus (NCB) Consider how accumulated NCB affects your renewal, as it can lead to increased coverage or premium discounts. This is super important while renewing medical insurance online.
Network Hospitals Confirm that the insurer’s network includes facilities easily accessible to you for a smooth cashless claim process. No point running to another city in case of health emergencies!
Exclusions and Waiting Periods Understand policy exclusions and waiting periods for specific treatments or conditions. If your health insurance provider doesn’t cover or has stopped covering a specific condition, you can consider switching.
Co-payment or Deductibles Check if the policy has co-payment or deductibles and understand your financial responsibilities. This is important for smart tax management.
Pre-Existing Conditions Verify how pre-existing conditions are covered after renewal and if waiting periods need to be fulfilled again
Additions or Changes Assess if you need to add new family members or make changes to coverage during renewal. Newly married individuals often forget to benefit from adding their spouse to the policy and keep paying double!
Claim Settlement History Check if your health insurance plan offers portability to seamlessly transfer benefits to a new insurer if your needs are not met.
Terms and Conditions Carefully read and understand health insurance policy terms and any changes made, ensuring agreement with them.
