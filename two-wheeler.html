Types of Two-Wheeler Insurance Policies in India

Third Party Liability

These policies fulfill the financial obligations arising from accidents involving third-party assets, as mandated by the Motor Vehicles Act of 1988. They cover damages or injuries caused to others by the insured two- wheeler.

Own-damage Cover

This type of policy provides financial protection specifically against theft or damage to the insured bike, scooter, activa, or ebike, offering peace of mind to the owner in case of such unfortunate events. One of the cheapest 2-wheeler insurance options out there!

Comprehensive Cover

Combining the benefits of third-party liability and own-damage coverage, comprehensive insurance provides a wider scope of protection. It safeguards against expenses arising from theft or damage to the insured vehicle as well as third-party liability.

Process of Filling Two-Wheeler Insurance Claims Online

Navigating the process of filing a claim for your two-wheeler bike insurance purchased through Aapka Policywala is straightforward and hassle-free. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

1

Inform the Insurer

Contact your insurance company or their customer service helpline following the accident or incident. Aapka Policywala’s dedicated claims specialist will assist you in providing detailed information, including the date, time, and location of the event.

2

Provide Necessary Information

Ensure you have your policy number, contact details, and incident details ready when reporting the claim. Include a description of the accident, extent of damage or injuries, and any involved third parties.

3

File a First Information Report (FIR)

If the incident involves theft, vandalism, or major accidents with injuries, file an FIR with the local police station. Keep a copy as it may be required during the claim process.

4

Submit Required Documents

Your insurer will request specific documents to support your claim. These may include a completed claim form, copy of your driving license, two-wheeler’s registration certificate, photographs of the damaged vehicle, repair estimates, medical reports (if applicable), and other relevant documents.

5

Claim Survey

Depending on the extent of damage, a surveyor may assess the loss. They will inspect your two-wheeler bike or scooter, verify claim details, and submit a report to the insurer.

6

Repair Your Vehicle

Opt for repairs at a network garage for cashless claims or choose a non-network garage for reimbursement claims. Submit repair bills accordingly.

7

Settlement

Once the surveyor’s report is received and all documents submitted, your claim will be processed. If approved, the insurer will settle the claim directly with the repair garage or reimburse you accordingly.

8

Claim Status

Stay updated on your bike, activa, or scooter claim’s progress by contacting customer service or checking online using our platform or the insurer’s website or mobile app.

Add-Ons for Enhanced Two-Wheeler Insurance

Sometimes, add-ons can serve as invaluable safeguards against financial loss and offer added security for your two-wheeler vehicle. Here are some common add-ons provided by insurers that are part of Aapka Policywala:

Zero Depreciation Cover: This add-on ensures you receive the full claim amount without factoring in depreciation on the vehicle’s parts during repairs. It is particularly beneficial for new or recently purchased bikes, scooters, ebike, or mopeds.

Engine Protection Cover: This cheap add-on provides coverage for damages to the engine resulting from water ingression, lubricating oil leakage, or gearbox damage. Since standard policies may exclude engine-related issues, this add-on can prove to be invaluable.

Roadside Assistance Cover: Roadside assistance cover offers 24/7 assistance for breakdowns, flat tires, battery related issues, fuel depletion, and more, providing reassurance during 2-wheeler emergencies and ensuring you’re never stranded on the road.

Consumable Expenses Cover: This add-on covers expenses on consumables like engine oil, nuts, bolts, and washers during repairs, alleviating the financial burden of these expenses during claim settlement and enhancing your overall coverage.

Return to Invoice (RTI) Cover: In the event of total loss or theft of your two-wheeler, RTI cover ensures you receive the original invoice value of the vehicle rather than the depreciated value, offering enhanced financial protection and peace of mind.

Personal Accident Cover for Pillion Rider: Personal accident cover for the pillion rider extends personal accident coverage to the pillion rider, offering financial protection in case of accidental death or disability, ensuring comprehensive coverage for all occupants.

Key Replacement Cover: This affordable add-on gives coverage for the cost of replacing lost or stolen keys of your two-wheeler, offering added convenience and security, ensuring you’re always prepared in case of key-related issues.
