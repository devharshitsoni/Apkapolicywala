Bharat Laghu Udyam Suraksha: Major Highlights

Understanding the intricacies of an insurance policy can feel overwhelming. But fret not, Aapka Policywala has prepared a list of key features of Bharat Laghu Udyam Suraksha. Here, this breakdown will make it easier for you to grasp its protective power:

Policy Duration

This insurance provides coverage for a period of up to 12months, ensuring your business remains shielded for a full year.

Sum Insured

The policy caters to businesses with a total value of assets exceeding Rs 5 crore but not surpassing Rs 50 crore. By defining the sum insured beforehand, you can make sure you receive the appropriatefinancial compensation in case of a covered incident.

In-Built Covers

Bharat Laghu Udyam Suraksha goes beyond the standard fire and special perils (SFSP) coverage. It will offer additional protection against unforeseen events. Basically, providing you with a big safety net for your business.

Standard Add-Ons

This policy listed on Aapka Policywala’s platform also offers the flexibility to add on valuable features like a floater for stocks and declaration policies to further customize your coverage and ensure specificneeds are addressed.

Basis of Sum Insured

In the unfortunate event of property damage, this policy will reimburse you based on the reinstatement value on a new for old basis. Meaning, you’ll receive the cost required to rebuild your property or replace damaged equipment with new ones.

Underinsurance Waiver (up to 15%)

Bharat Laghu Suraksha insurance policy acts as a buffer. It will protect you from a situation where the insured value is slightly lower than the actual value of your assets. Up to a 15% discrepancy won’t affect your claim payout. (Note: This waiver does not apply to specifically covered items like money,important documents, etc.)

Excess

A small excess amount of 5% of the claim amount (minimum Rs.10,000) will be applicable for each claim. This amount is borne by you before the insurance company settles the remaining claim amount. Coverage and Exclusions of Laghu Udyam Insurance

Okay, we believe that a transparent approach to insurance is key. Here’s a breakdown of what’s included and excluded in the Bharat Laghu Udyam Suraksha (BLUS) policy offered by Aapka Policywala:

Building and Structures: This encompasses your business premises, including basements, essential fixtures and fittings, garages, towers, security sheds, internal roadways, elevators, air conditioning systems, security systems, electrical installations, fire sprinkler systems, and any other permanent structures within the insured location.

Inventory and Stock: Bharat Laghu Udyam Suraksha policy will also help you safeguard your finished goods, semi-finished goods, stock in progress, raw materials, packaging materials, and other types of commodities or merchandise that are essential for your business operations.

Machinery and Equipment: This insurance policy covers furniture, office equipment (including electronics), canteen and kitchen equipment, computers, and other valuable assets that keep your business running smoothly.

What's Not Covered Under Bharat Griha Raksha Insurance?

Intentional Acts: The policy doesn’t cover losses or damages arising from deliberate actions, willful negligence, or intentional misconduct on your part.

Missing or Mislaid Property: Bharat Laghu Udyam Suraksha policy doesn’t compensate for property that goes missing without a clear explanation or has been simply misplaced.

Indirect Losses: The policy excludes coverage for consequential or indirect losses that may stem from a covered event. For example, if a fire damages your equipment and diminishes your business opportunities, that wouldn’t be covered.

Temperature-Related Losses: The policy excludes coverage for consequential or indirect losses that may stem from a covered event. For example, if a fire damages your equipment and diminishes your business opportunities, that wouldn’t be covered.

Unoccupied Property: If your insured premises remain unoccupied for more than 30 consecutive days, the property may be considered abandoned and coverage may cease to apply.

Specific Valuables: Unless explicitly mentioned in an add-on cover, Bharat Laghu Udyam Suraksha insurance policy doesn’t cover bullion, unset precious stones, oddities, or works of art. These valuable items may require separate insurance coverage.

Electrical/Electronic Machinery Breakdown: Standard Bharat Laghu Udyam Suraksha coverage doesn’t extend to damage caused by the breakdown of electrical or electronic machinery due to wear and tear or inherent malfunctions.

Filing a Laghu Udyam Suraksha Insurance Claim

Experiencing a loss at your business can be stressful. Aapka Policywala is here to guide you through the claims process of your Bharat Laghu Udyam Suraksha insurance to ensure a smooth and swift resolution. Here’s what you need to do:

Notify Us Immediately

Contact Aapka Policywala as soon as possible after a covered incident occurs. The sooner we are informed, the faster we can begin assisting you with the claims process.

Report to Authorities

It’s important to file reports with the relevant authorities such as the police or fire department (depending on the nature of the incident). This documentation will be essential for your claim. Minimise Further Damage: Take all reasonable steps to prevent further damage to your property after the initial incident. This might involve securing the premises, boarding broken windows, and taking steps to prevent water damage from spreading.

Document the Damage

Take clear photographs and videos of the damaged property and surrounding areas. This visual evidence will be crucial in supporting your claim of Bharat Laghu Udyam Suraksha insurance policy!

Gather Necessary Documents

Start collecting any relevant documents related to the incident such as police reports, repair estimates, and receipts for any temporary repairs undertaken.

Submit a Claim Form Support

Aapka Policywala or its POSP agent will provide you with a claim form. Fill it out accurately and completely. Include all details about the incident and the extent of the damage.

Cooperate with Us

We understand this can be a challenging time. But hey, your cooperation is essential for us! Provide us with all requested information, documents, and access to the damaged property (if necessary) to facilitate a smooth claims investigation.

Timely Action is Key

It’s important to submit your completed claim form within 30 days of noticing the loss or damage. This ensures your claim is processed efficiently and avoids any delays.
