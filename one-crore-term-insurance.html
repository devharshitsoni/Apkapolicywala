How’s Sum Assured Calculated in a Term Plan?

Understanding how your sum assured is calculated in a 1 crore term insurance plan online can feel like piecing together a puzzle. But it’s not that difficult. Sum assured basically means the cost of coverage or financial protection that your family (inheritors) will receive after your death during policy term.Put simply, it’s the core benefit of a term insurance policy. Also, one of the primary aspects that define the policy’s premium. Here’s a breakdown:

Income

Think of your annual income as the cornerstone. Typically, your sum assured is pegged at 10 to 20 times your annual earnings. It's like building a house that matches your financial foundation.

Financial Responsibilities

Consider your outstanding debts and future commitments. Whether it's clearing loans or funding your children's dreams, your sum assured should buffer these financial obligations.

Lifestyle & Livelihood

Factor in your family's day-to-day needs and aspirations. Your sum assured should be a lifeline that sustains their lifestyle. It should make sure they don't miss a beat if you're no longer there to provide.

Age & Health

Your vitality matters. Younger, healthier individuals often qualify for more coverage at lower costs. Secure maximum protection while you're in your prime and get a 1 crore term insurance cover from Aapka Policywala!

Inflation's Impact

Okay, picture growing expenses over time. Your sum assured should be a safeguard against the eroding power of inflation. Basically, ensuring it remains relevant in the face of evolving financial landscapes.

Existing Coverage

If you're already covered elsewhere, adjust accordingly. Your sum assured should complement but not duplicate existing insurance provisions and also monthly/yearly premium expenses. This is especially true because 1 crore insurance has a decent premium.
