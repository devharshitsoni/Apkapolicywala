Hull and Machinery Insurance for Ships

As a crucial aspect of maritime risk management, Hull and Machinery Insurance safeguards vessels and their equipment against various perils, ensuring the smooth operation and protection of maritime assets. Whether you run a single ship or a fleet, our hull insurance plans will give you the protection you need.

What is Hull and Machinery Insurance?

Hull and Machinery Insurance is a specialized type of marine insurance that provides coverage for physical damage to ships or vessels and their equipment. It is designed to protect shipowners, operators, and other stakeholders fromfinancial losses resulting from various risks encountered during the operation of maritime vessels.

At its core, Hull and Machinery Insurance covers the hull (the main body of the vessel), machinery (engines, boilers, turbines, etc.), and other equipment essential for the operation of the vessel. This coverage extends to a wide range of perils, including but not limited to, collisions, grounding, stranding, sinking, fire, explosion, and natural disasters such as storms or hurricanes. Additionally, it may also include coverage for salvage costs, towing expenses, and general average contributions incurred during a covered event. Make sure you buy one from a trusted online insurance company in India to get all the benefits of this hull insurance plan.

What are the Benefits of Hull Insurance?

Hull and Machinery (H&M) Insurance offers a multitude of benefits to shipowners, operators, and other stakeholders in the maritime industry, providing crucial financial protection and peace of mind in the face of various risks and uncertainties.

Financial protection against physical damage

In the event of accidents, collisions, groundings, or other covered perils, the insurance policy covers the costs associated with repairing or replacing damaged hulls, machinery, and equipment. This helps mitigate the potentially substantial financial losses that shipowners and operators could face without adequate insurance coverage.

Flexibility in tailoring coverage

To meet the specific needs of different types of vessels and their operations. Policies can be customized based on factors such as the type and size of the vessel, its operating area, and the level of risk exposure, ensuring that shipowners receive adequate protection that aligns with their unique requirements.

Includes coverage for additional expenses

Expenses incurred during covered incidents, such as salvage costs, towing expenses, and general average contributions. This comprehensive coverage not only protects the vessel itself but also extends to ancillary expenses associated with mitigating losses and ensuring the safe recovery of the vessel and its cargo.
