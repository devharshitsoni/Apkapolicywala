Understanding Passenger Carrying Vehicle Insurance

Also known as commercial passenger vehicle insurance, passenger-carrying vehicle insurance is a specialized type of coverage that is crafted to safeguard vehicles engaged in transporting passengers. This includes buses, mini buses, taxis, and other public transport vehicles. Passenger carrying vehicle insurance policy has a wide coverage. It shields passengers, drivers, and vehicle proprietors against financial setbacks in the event of accidents, injuries, or damages, encompassing various vital components and ensuring comprehensive protection. What Does Passenger Carrying Vehicle Insurance Exclude?

Although passenger-carrying vehicle insurance policies listed on Aapka Policy Wala indeed offer extensive coverage against various risks, certain exclusions exist within them. These exclusions may vary depending on the insurance provider and specific policy terms.

Here are common exclusions typically found in online passenger carrying vehicle insurance:

General Wear and Tear

Damages resulting from the natural wear and tear of the vehicle over time are not covered.

Mechanical or Electrical Breakdown

Damages arising from mechanical or electrical breakdowns of the vehicle are excluded from coverage.

Intentional Acts

Damages caused by intentional acts or deliberate negligence of driver or owner are not covered.

Driving Under the Influence

Any damages or accidents occurring while the driver is under alcohol or drug influence are not covered.

Unauthorized Use

If the vehicle is operated by an unauthorised driver or used for illegal purposes, insurance coverage may be void.

Consequential Loss

Losses or damages indirectly caused by the initial covered event may be excluded from coverage.

War and Terrorism

Damages caused due to war, terrorism, or nuclear events will be expunged from the policy.

Racing or Adventure Sports

Accidents that occur during racing, stunts, or adventure sports activities are typically not covered.

How to File a Claim for Passenger Carrying Vehicle Insurance?

Aapka Policywala understands that filing a claim can be a daunting task. That’s why we’ve streamlined the process to ensure a seamless and efficient settlement. Here’s a step-by-step guide tailored for our valued customers:

1

Notify Aapka Policywala

As soon as the incident occurs, reach out to us through our dedicated helpline or email. We’re here to assist you every step of the way.

2

Provide Essential Details

During the notification, provide crucial information like your policy number, date and time of the incident, location, description of the event, and the extent of damages or injuries.

3

File an FIR

In cases involving theft, burglary, or criminal activities, file a First Information Report (FIR) at the nearest police station and obtain a copy for documentation.

4

Document the Scene

Capture the accident site, damages, injuries, and any relevant evidence through photographs if possible. This documentation serves as vital support for your claim.

5

Seek Medical Attension

Prioritize the well-being of passengers or third parties by seeking immediate medical assistance if injuries are involved. Include all medical records and bills to make the claim process snappy.

6

Inform the RTO

For significant accidents or damages, notify the Regional Transport Office (RTO) about the incident. Especially if there are fatalities or substantial property damage.

7

Complete the Claim Form

Obtain and fill out the claim form available on our website. Make sure all information provided is accurate and detailed to expedite the claim settlement process.

8

Submit Necessary Documents

Attach all required documents including the claim form, FIR copy, policy document, driver’s licence, vehicle registration, and any other relevant paperwork.

9

Await Inspection

Aapka Policywala may perform a survey or assessment of the vehicle. This will help us evaluate damages and ascertain the claim. During the process, our team will coordinate with you seamlessly.

10

Cooperate with Investigation

Cooperate with any investigation conducted by our team or insurance firm. Your cooperation expedites the claim resolution process.

11

Stay Updated

Keep track of your claim’s progress through our website or by contacting our customer support team. We’re intent on keeping you apprised every step of the way!
