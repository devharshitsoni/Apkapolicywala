What's Covered Under Bharat Griha Raksha Insurance?

Bharat Griha Raksha policy offers comprehensive protection for your home, encompassing two key areas:

Home Building Cover: This acts as the foundation of your policy. It safeguards the physical structure of your home. This includes walls, roof, floors, and permanent fixtures.

Home Contents Cover: This layer of protection also extends to the valuables within your home. Here’s how it works:

  • Automatic Coverage: Up to 20% of the insured building sum (with a maximum limit of Rs. 10 lakh) automatically covers your general home contents.
  • Enhanced Coverage (Optional): If you have valuable possessions exceeding the automatic coverage limit, you can opt for a higher sum insured for home contents. This option allows you to declare specific items for additional protection.
 

What's Not Covered Under Bharat Griha Raksha Insurance?

While Bharat Griha Raksha plan offers extensive coverage, there are certain exclusions to be aware of:

Willful Damage: The policy doesn’t cover losses caused intentionally or through deliberate negligence on your part.

War and Warlike Activities: Losses arising from war, invasion, or military operations aren’t covered.

Nuclear Activities: Damage caused by radiation or nuclear activity falls outside the scope of this policy.

Missing or Misplaced Property: The policy doesn’t compensate for lost or misplaced items without a clear explanation.

Government Action: Losses due to government actions such as confiscation or demolition aren’t covered.
