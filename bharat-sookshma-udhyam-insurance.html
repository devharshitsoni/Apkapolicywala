What is Bharat Sookshma Udyam Suraksha Policy?

Bharat Sookshma Udhyam Suraksha (BSUS) policy is a government-backed insurance scheme designed specifically for small and medium-sized shopkeepers. Recognizing the important role these businesses play in the Indian economy, this initiative aims to provide financial security and safeguard them against unforeseen events. BSUS insurance offers complete coverage for your shop’s physical assets including the building and its contents. This can include inventory, furniture, fixtures, and even machinery (depending on your specific shop setup.) By opting for this scheme, you gain peace of mind knowing that your business is protected against various potential risks.You can focus on serving your customers and keeping your shop thriving! Make sure you buy one from a trusted online insurance site in India to get all the benefits of this government insurance plan for micro businesses.

Who Can Benefit from This Government Insurance Policy?

As we mentioned before, Bharat Sookshma Udhyam Suraksha insurance policy is specifically designed to cater to small and medium-sized shopkeepers in India. However, your business needs to meet the following criteria to be eligible for this insurance by government:

Value of Business Assets: The total value of your shop’s assets (including the building, inventory, and equipment must not exceed ₹5 crore at the time of policy purchase.

In-Built Covers – Property Ownership: You can be eligible for BSUS insurance if you are:

  • The owner of the place you use for your shop.
  • A tenant or lessee who is legally permitted to operate a commercial business on the premises.

Aapka Policywala strives to provide transparency in insurance coverage. Here’s a breakdown of what’s included and excluded in the Bharat Sookshma Udhyam Suraksha policy:

Covered Events (Inclusions) 

  1. Fire and Allied Perils: This includes damage caused by fire, lightning, explosion, and implosion.
  2. Natural Disasters: BSUS covers losses arising from events like earthquakes, floods, storms, and landslides
  3. Impact Damage:  Coverage extends to damage caused by the impact of a motor vehicle, aircraft, or any external object.
  4.  Riots, Strikes, and Malicious Acts: The policy covers damage caused by riots, strikes, and other acts of vandalism.

Covered Events (Inclusions) 

  1. Breakdown and Wear & Tear: Bharat Sookshma Udyam Suraksha policy doesn’t cover damage caused by the wear and tear of equipment, machinery breakdown, or electrical/mechanical malfunctions.
  2. Construction Activities: Any damage arising from alterations, renovation, or excavation near your shop is excluded.
  3. Owned Vehicle Damage:  Damage caused by a vehicle belonging to you, your employees, or anyone associated with the insured shop is not covered.
  4. Illegal Activities: The policy excludes losses resulting from illegal activities conducted on the insured property.

This is a general overview! Specific inclusions and exclusions may vary depending on the chosen BSUS plan and insurance provider.
